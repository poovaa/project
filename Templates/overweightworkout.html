Workout Plan for Weight Loss

General Guidelines

  • Combination of Cardio and Strength Training: Aim for at least 150-300 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise per week.
  • Incorporate Strength Training: Helps build muscle, which can boost metabolism.

Sample Workout Plan

Day 1: Full Body Strength

  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Push-Ups: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (modified if needed)
  • Bent-Over Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds

Day 2: Cardio

30-45 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio (brisk walking, cycling, swimming)

Day 3: Upper Body Strength

  • Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Day 4: Rest or Active Recovery

Light yoga, stretching, or a leisurely walk

Day 5: Lower Body Strength

  • Deadlifts (lightweight): 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
  • Leg Press or Step-Ups: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Calf Raises: 4 sets of 12-15 reps

Day 6: High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

20-30 minutes of HIIT (alternating between 30 seconds of high effort and 1 minute of low effort)

Day 7: Flexibility or Rest

Focus on stretching, yoga, or foam rolling for recovery

Additional Tips

  • Track Your Progress: Keep a food diary and log your workouts to monitor your intake and activity.
  • Be Patient: Weight loss takes time; aim for sustainable changes rather than quick fixes.
