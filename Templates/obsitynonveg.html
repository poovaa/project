Non-Vegetarian Diet Plan for Weight Loss

General Guidelines

  • Caloric Deficit: Aim to consume 500-1,000 fewer calories than your maintenance level for a safe weight loss of about 1-2 pounds per week.
  • Nutrient-Dense Foods: Focus on whole, minimally processed foods.
  • Hydration: Drink plenty of water, aiming for at least 8 cups a day.

Sample Meal Plan

Breakfast

  • Omelette made with 2-3 eggs, spinach, and tomatoes
  • A slice of whole grain toast

Morning Snack

  • Greek yogurt (low-fat) with a handful of berries

Lunch

  • Grilled chicken breast with a large mixed green salad (lettuce, cucumbers, tomatoes) topped with olive oil and vinegar

Afternoon Snack

  • Sliced turkey or chicken breast wrapped in lettuce

Dinner

  • Baked fish (like salmon or tilapia) with steamed broccoli and quinoa

Evening Snack (optional)

  • Cottage cheese with pineapple or berries
