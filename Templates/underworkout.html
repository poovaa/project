Workout Plan for Muscle Gain

General Guidelines

  • Focus on Strength Training: Aim for 4-5 days per week, targeting all major muscle groups.
  • Compound Exercises: Prioritize exercises that work multiple muscle groups.

Sample Workout Plan

Day 1: Upper Body

  • Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Bent-Over Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Overhead Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Pull-Ups (or assisted): 3 sets of 5-8 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 2: Lower Body

  • Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Deadlifts (Romanian or Conventional): 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Calf Raises: 4 sets of 12-15 reps

Day 3: Rest or Active Recovery

Day 4: Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets to failure
  • Push-Ups: 3 sets to failure

Day 5: Pull (Back, Biceps)

  • Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Seated Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Face Pulls: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 6: Legs

  • Front Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Leg Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Bulgarian Split Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps per leg

Day 7: Rest or Light Activity

Additional Tips

  • Hydration: Drink plenty of fluids, opting for calorie-rich options like smoothies.
  • Track Progress: Keep a log of meals and workouts to ensure consistent caloric surplus and strength gains.
