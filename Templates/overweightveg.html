Vegetarian Diet Plan for Weight Loss

General Guidelines

  • Caloric Deficit: Aim to consume fewer calories than you burn.
  • Nutrient-Dense Foods: Focus on whole, minimally processed foods.
  • Hydration: Drink plenty of water, aiming for at least 8 cups a day.

Sample Meal Plan

Breakfast

Smoothie with spinach, banana, a scoop of protein powder, and unsweetened almond milk

Morning Snack

Carrot and cucumber sticks with hummus

Lunch

Quinoa salad with chickpeas, cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, and lemon dressing

Afternoon Snack

A small handful of nuts (almonds or walnuts) and an apple

Dinner

Stir-fried tofu with mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots) served over brown rice

Evening Snack (optional)

Air-popped popcorn or a small fruit salad
