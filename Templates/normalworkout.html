Sample Workout Plan

Day 1: Full Body Strength

Squats: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Push-Ups: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Bent-Over Rows: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Plank: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds

Day 2: Cardio

30-45 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio (running, cycling, swimming, or brisk walking)

Day 3: Upper Body Strength

Bench Press or Chest Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Day 4: Rest or Active Recovery

Light yoga, stretching, or a leisurely walk

Day 5: Lower Body Strength

Deadlifts: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Lunges: 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg

Leg Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Calf Raises: 4 sets of 12-15 reps

Day 6: Cardio or HIIT

20-30 minutes of high-intensity interval training (sprints, circuit training)

Day 7: Flexibility or Rest

Focus on stretching, yoga, or foam rolling for recovery

Additional Tips

Consistency: Stick to your routine to see results over time.

Listen to Your Body: Adjust intensity and rest as needed.

Variety: Change up your meals and workouts regularly to avoid boredom.
