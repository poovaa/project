Non-Vegetarian Meal Plan

General Guidelines

  • Balanced Meals: Focus on a mix of protein, healthy fats, and carbohydrates.
  • Hydration: Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Meal Timing: Aim for 3 main meals and 2-3 snacks daily.

Sample Meal Plan

Breakfast

Scrambled eggs (2-3 eggs) with spinach and tomatoes

Whole grain toast with avocado

Morning Snack

Greek yogurt with a drizzle of honey and a handful of granola

Lunch

Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and vinaigrette

Quinoa or brown rice on the side

Afternoon Snack

Sliced turkey or chicken breast rolled with cheese

Dinner

Baked salmon or chicken breast with roasted sweet potatoes and steamed broccoli

Evening Snack (optional)

Cottage cheese with pineapple or berries
